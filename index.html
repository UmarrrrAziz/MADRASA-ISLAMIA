About Our Institute

MADRASA ISLAMIA NOOR UL ULOOM is a respected Islamic educational institute situated in Rashanpora Kralpora, India. The institute is dedicated to spreading the light of Islamic knowledge, moral values, discipline, and spiritual guidance among students and the local community.

The Madarsa aims to provide quality Islamic education along with proper character building so students can grow with strong faith, good manners, and a sense of responsibility towards society. Special attention is given to Quranic education, Islamic teachings, basic Arabic learning, and moral upbringing.

Under the leadership of Mufti Abdul Aziz Beigh Qasmi, the institute continues to serve the community with sincerity, dedication, and commitment towards education and social welfare.

The Madarsa also encourages unity, respect, honesty, and compassion among students while preserving Islamic traditions and values for future generations.

Located in the peaceful area of Rashanpora Kralpora, the institute provides a positive and disciplined environment for students to learn, grow, and excel both spiritually and academically.
